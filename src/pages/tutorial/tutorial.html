Welcome to Talkable

10 weeks of language learning fun!

Unique content each week

Each week a video tutorial will guide you through a new strategy, helping you to create a rich language learning environment at home.

Learn to Sign

Each week you will learn 4 new key word hand signs selected from the most common words used by toddlers.

By the end of the program you will be able to use 40 hand signs with your little one.

Track your progress

The Word Tracker function allows you to keep track of your child's growing vocabulary.

This is a great way to measure your child's language learning.

Let's get started!
